## Design Context

### Users
The primary audience consists of students (from kindergarten to high school), parents, and teachers. They are visiting to seek and purchase educational materials, school books, and resources tailored to specific educational stages. 

### Brand Personality
- **3-Word Personality:** Trustworthy, Clear, Encouraging
- **Voice & Tone:** Calm, supportive, secure, and friendly.
- **Emotional Goals:** Parents should feel secure and confident in their purchases; students and teachers should feel welcomed and encouraged. 

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual Tone:** Clean, calm, and uncluttered. It should prioritize clarity to easily navigate educational stages. 
- **Theme:** Predominantly Light Mode to maintain a clear and inviting aesthetic, using Teal (#4EC4BD) as a calming primary base and Golden (#FFD43B) accents to add warmth and friendliness.
- **Typography:** 'Cairo' font for highly legible and friendly Arabic typography.

### Design Principles
1. **Clarity over Clutter:** Keep navigation through educational stages intuitive. Avoid unnecessary visual noise so users can quickly locate specific books.
2. **Warmth & Encouragement:** Use the golden accent intentionally to highlight important actions, making the learning journey feel inviting rather than clinical.
3. **Instill Trust:** Maintain a polished, professional structural layout that reassures parents of the brand's reliability.
4. **Legible & Accessible:** Ensure high contrast for text (especially using the Cairo font) so it’s easily readable for users of all ages, from young children to older adults.
